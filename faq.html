Frequently Asked Questions

How does the ride matching algorithm work?
Our backend database checks your selected Source and Destination against active "Forming" ride groups. It uses a 15-minute overlapping time window algorithm to group you with users heading the same way.
How is the fare calculated?
The total fare is retrieved from integrated platforms like InDrive or Careem based on distance. SyncRide then automatically splits the total fare evenly among all group members in the ride. You can view Fare Estimates in your Dashboard.
Can I choose my preferred gender for carpooling?
Yes. In your user dashboard under Profile Management, you can toggle "Preferred Gender Matching" to ensure you are only matched with users of your own gender.
What happens if a driver cancels?
If a driver cancels, the ride group status returns to "Forming", and the SyncRide backend automatically pings the integrated platforms to re-assign a new driver without the group needing to create a new request.
