About SyncRide

Our Mission

SyncRide was founded on a simple principle: commuting shouldn't be a solitary, expensive, or environmentally damaging experience. Our mission is to connect people traveling the same routes at the same times, reducing traffic congestion, splitting costs, and fostering a sense of community among commuters.

How It Works

We utilize an advanced time-coordinated matching algorithm. When you enter your destination and expected departure time, SyncRide instantly checks our database for users with overlapping routes within a ±15-minute window. By simulating integration with top platforms like Yango, Careem, InDrive, and Bykea, we ensure the best possible ride group formation.

Why Choose Us?

  • Cost Effective: Fares are automatically split.
  • Eco-Friendly: Fewer cars on the road means lower carbon emissions.
  • Smart Routing: Optimized pick-up and drop-off sequences.
