Your Safety is Our Priority

Verified Users Only

Every user on SyncRide must pass a phone and email verification check before they can request or join a ride. Our Admin team continually monitors user activity to ensure community guidelines are followed.

Preferred Gender Matching

We offer a feature in your dashboard profile allowing you to request ride groups strictly comprising users of the same gender. We respect your comfort and boundaries during transit.

Live Ride Tracking

Through our simulated platform integrations, your ride group's route is monitored. If a vehicle deviates significantly from the algorithm's route, safety alerts are triggered to our administration panel.

Driver Vetting

Drivers are pulled from reputable platforms like Yango, Careem, and Bykea. We cross-reference their ratings, and anyone dropping below a 4.0 average rating is automatically excluded from our matching pool.
